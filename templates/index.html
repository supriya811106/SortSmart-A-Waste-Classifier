Types of Waste Materials

Battery


Batteries have harmful stuff inside. Throwing them away can hurt our environment. It's important to recycle them at special places to keep our world clean.

Cardboard


Cardboard can be made into new things if we don't throw it away. When it ends up in big piles, it's bad for the Earth. So, remember to put it in the recycling bin.

Clothes


Old clothes should go to charity or places that recycle them. It's not good because it makes air pollution. Let's help others and the Earth by giving away clothes.

Glass


Glass can hurt animals and look bad in our neighborhoods if it's not recycled. We should recycle glass bottles and jars by putting them in the right bin.

Metal


Metal stuff can be used again and again, but it's not good for the Earth if we throw it away. Let's recycle metal to save energy and keep our planet clean.

Organic


Organic waste like food and plants can make a bad gas in landfills. Composting it is like magic for the Earth. It helps the soil and stops the bad gas.

Paper


Paper can be turned into new things, but if it's not recycled, trees are cut down. We can help forests and keep our air clean by recycling paper.

Plastic


Plastic things can stay in the environment for a very long time and hurt animals. Let's recycle plastic and make sure it's clean so we can protect our planet.

Shoes


Don't throw away old shoes. If they are still good, give them to someone who needs them. If not, find a recycling place for shoes to keep them out of the trash.

Trash


When we throw everything in the trash, it can cause problems for our world. Only put stuff in the trash when we can't do anything else with it.
